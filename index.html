Problem Statements

Drones and IoT in Agriculture

Farmers face difficulty monitoring large fields for crop health pest infestation irrigation and nutrients. A drone and IoT based system is needed to collect real time farm data analyze crop conditions and provide recommendations for precision farming.

First Prize ₹3000

Designing Farm Implements

Small farmers lack affordable ergonomic farm tools for land preparation planting weeding and landscaping. There is a need to design innovative low cost energy efficient implements suitable for small farms and uneven terrain.

First Prize ₹3000

Water Quality Management

Poor irrigation water affects soil fertility crop growth and food safety. Farmers need a system that monitors irrigation water quality detects contaminants and provides real time guidance to protect soil health.
